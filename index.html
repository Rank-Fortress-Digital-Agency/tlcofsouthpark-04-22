Making Rocket Ships

Read Post
Visit Us

This week during our space project our kids will be learning about space travel. We will look at rocket ships, how they move and look on the inside. We will talk about the cone shape of the front of the air craft and how that helps move faster. Keeping in mind the shape of the cone, the children will use different recycled materials to create their own rocket ship.

Next week we will be learning about astronauts and how they live on the rocket ships.
